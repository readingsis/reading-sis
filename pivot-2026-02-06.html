About the show
›
Pivot is a weekly podcast where Kara Swisher and Scott Galloway dissect the week's biggest business and tech stories with sharp analysis and insider perspective. The hosts break down corporate strategy, media disruption, and the personalities shaping Silicon Valley and beyond, offering listeners a front-row seat to the forces transforming the digital economy.
Takeaways
1
Anthropic plays Super Bowl branding game strategically Anthropic's surprise Super Bowl ad directly challenged OpenAI, demonstrating how even in AI's crowded landscape, bold brand positioning can cut through noise. This move capitalizes on consumer interest in the AI narrative while establishing Anthropic as a credible alternative to market leader OpenAI.
2
Google's dominance in advertising remains unshakeable Alphabet's blockbuster earnings underscore the continued strength of search and digital advertising, even as competition intensifies. The company's financial performance reveals how entrenched its market position remains despite regulatory scrutiny and emerging competitors.
3
Media layoffs signal structural decline in newsrooms The Washington Post's decision to cut a third of its workforce reflects broader economic pressures facing legacy media, as subscription and advertising models struggle. These cuts have major implications for journalism quality and newsroom capacity during a critical political and cultural moment.
